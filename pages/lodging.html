Hotel Accommodation and Travel

Hotel Accommodation

Conference Hotel

  • The Chelsea Hotel Toronto Downtown is the official hotel for the conference.
  • The hotel is in Toronto's downtown core, a 25-minute walk from the Daniels Building (conference venue) and a 5-minute walk from the Dundas metro station.
  • Room rate: The special rate of $239+tax CAD/night will be available until June 23rd or until the ICCP room block is sold out. The rate is valid for stays between July 20th and July 24th. The rate includes WiFi.
  • To book a room: Use this booking link or call the hotel at 1-800-243-5732 and give them the booking code ICC072125.
  • If you wish to book a longer stay (i.e., before July 20th or after July 24th), we recommend you book the extra days separately in order to take advantage of ICCP's preferential rate for the dates of the conference.

Knox College

  • A small block of rooms has been reserved at UofT's Knox residence, a beautiful student residence in the heart of the University of Toronto's downtown campus.
  • Knox College is across the street from the Bahen Building (summer school venue) and a 5-minute walk from the Daniels Building (conference venue).
  • Room rate: $62.5 CAD for single-occupancy rooms with a shared, dormitory-style bathroom. Rate includes Wi-Fi.
  • To book a room: Rooms are specifically reserved for students of the ICCP summer school. Booking instructions will be sent to successful applicants to the summer school.

Getting to Toronto

Visa and/or Electronic Travel Authorization

  • Visitors to Canada may need to apply for an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) or a visitor visa.
  • Attendees who need to apply for a visitor visa should start the application process as soon as possible. Please refer to this page for current visa application processing times for your location.
  • We only issue invitation letters to people who have already registered. See the registration page for more information.

By plane

  • Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ). This airport serves major destinations in the Northeastern US and Canada, as well as Chicago. It is located at downtown Toronto's waterfront and is particularly convenient for getting to/from the UofT campus (3.5km, 35min by streetcar, ~20min by car) and the conference hotel (4.5km, ~20min by car). A free shuttle takes passengers to Toronto's Union Station, which is a 10min subway ride from Chelsea Hotel and UofT. Because of its relatively limited incoming/outgoing flights, security checkpoints and Canadian customs processing are very fast and efficient.

By train/bus

Toronto's Union Station is the main hub for trains and long-distance buses.

  • VIA Rail and Amtrak connect Toronto to cities like Montreal, Ottawa, and New York.
  • Go Transit and Megabus connect Toronto to cities like Montreal, Ottawa, and New York.

Local transportation

  • The TTC operates subways, streetcars, and buses throughout Toronto. You can tap your credit/debit card or a PRESTO card to pay as you enter the station.
