Conference and Banquet Venues

Conference Venue

The conference will take place in the Daniels Building.

Getting to the Daniels Building from Chelsea Hotel: Walk west on Gerrard Street, then turn north on University Avenue. Continue west along College Street, cross Spadina Crescent, and arrive at the Daniels Building. The walk is approximately 25 minutes. Alternatively, take the subway from Dundas Station to Spadina Station, then walk south for 10 minutes.

Getting to the Daniels Building from Knox College: Walk west on Gerrard Street, turn right on University Avenue, then left on College Street. Continue west on College, turn right on Spadina Avenue, then left on Spadina Crescent. The Daniels Building will be on your left. The total walk is approximately 25 minutes.

School Venue

The school will take place in the Bahen Centre for Information Technology room BA3200.
