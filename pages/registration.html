For papers accepted to ICCP 2025, at least one author per paper must purchase registration. Please see the conference website at https://iccp-conference.org/ for additional details.

Refund Policy

All refund requests must be made before June 20, 2025, 23:59 ET. No refunds will be made after that time. Refunds will be subject to a $50 CAD processing fee. Please send refund requests via email to ICCP2025Finance@gmail.com. Please see below about refund requests relating to visa issues.

We are happy to change the name on the registration for no additional fee, assuming the change is within the same category (for example, IEEE member to another IEEE member)

Visa Letters

We only issue visa letters of invitation to people who have registered.

If you require an invitation, please complete your registration. You will receive a confirmation email upon successful registration. Forward the confirmation email to ICCP2025Finance@gmail.com stating that you require an invitation letter.

If your visa is denied for any reason and you are unable to attend in person, please contact us. We will issue a full refund of the registration fee unless you choose to participate remotely.
